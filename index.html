The aim of this portal is to create the web service, which enables online consultation with doctors from the whole world. Thus the cause of patient's disease could be found, rather than masking symptoms, what the common life shows us. Patient shouldn't take quantum of drugs. Instead of that, the targeted therapy for the cause of disease should be used.

This project was my wife's idea, who works as a pharmacist. She often tels me stories of people, who come to the pharmacy, to buy their drugs. The interesting thing which these patients have in common, is that their doctors treat symptoms instead of the cause of their diseases. What is it? Remember Dr. House.

The problem of our society is disinterest of doctors to find the cause of ssss disease and the effective treatment for it. The good example is the story of our friend. One day, she started to have big problems with her health. Suddenly, she had tachycardy, anxiety, weakness, shaking, palsy, swelter, insomnia. She was recommended to cardiologist, who prescribed her Beta-blockers (drugs which slow down the heart's activity). But her health didn't get better, because this drugs doesn't solve the cause of the disease, but they mask the symptoms. She visited many other doctors, who unfortunatelly also did not find out the cause of the disease. She was very exhausted from this condition. The solution came one day, when she was on preventive inspection at her doctor, which is very good diagnostic. She had the atack of tachycardy and discomfort. When the doctor saw it, she realised immediately, that the problem was, the imbalance of serotonine in brain, and with this diagnosis, she recommended her to the psychiatrist. This diagnosis was comfirmed and she prescribed her SSRI drugs (Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor). All symptoms stopped. For a time she took this drugs, and after the consultation with psychiatrist, the doses got lower. Today she doesnt take any drugs and she is healthy. If the doctor didn't find out the cause of our friend's problems, it could have letal ending.

My wife meets very often with causes like this. It is not rarity if patient has prescribed 15 different drugs, while at least five are unnecessary. Or they only hide side effects of the rest of drugs. This leads to the vicious circle. It would be sufficient to reveal cause of the disease and to treat patients effectively with for example 5 drugs. It would be also the big benefit for our healthcare.
